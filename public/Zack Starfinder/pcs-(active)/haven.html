Background & History

  • Who were you before The Scarring began?
    • A pro esports player, technically, but tried to hide previous involvement with criminal orgs. It’s not hard to tell what he used to do, though. When he did an occasional stream, it became a popular joke amongst his chat on what he used to do: hired thug, drug trafficking, assassination work, all were thrown out as suggestions.
    • He grew up in Denpakant with the name Vihe (v-eye-hey) Nanse Jaltra of Clan Runsa with his dad (Nel Samsal Teadri of Clan Runsa), but once he was old enough to start earning some credits for simple work, his dad needed more and more money for his growing gambling addiction. After shit hit the fan one too many times, Haven left their apartment at around 15 for a grimey, tiny one rented to him dirt cheap as long as he acted as errand boy for his landlord, Luthien. Turns out, he’d unknowingly joined a gang.
  • Were you connected to any gangs, guilds, noble houses, religious orders, militias, or other organizations before all this?
    • A local off-shoot of a gang that called themselves the Storm. He always thought it was a stupid and cliche name, but they seemed proud of themselves, and he didn’t really want to get his ass beat.
  • What did you do for them?
    • It started off small; make deliveries, debt collect, don’t die. Then as leadership slowly left or got axed, his roles got more important (mostly killing people who pissed off the upper guys), until he had a small squad of guys who listened to what he had to say. And as their influence grew, that meant more people died under his watch, even some of the new guys that he said he’d take care of. Being stuck in there for just about a decade, he had his fill and decided he’d had enough. So, he cut his losses (not without some yelling and a few new bullet scars in his right arms), moved across town, changed his name, and didn’t really touch the scene until Ithax started poking his head into things. He’s lucky he’s got someone like Haven there to keep an eye on him.
  • What is your strongest memory from before The Scarring? Has it changed in meaning since then?
    • The first time something made him falter in his actions. He was barely 17, still a nobody, but trust was put in him to get Luthien’s money from a “tenant” that was refusing to pay. It was a vesk woman and her teenage son, just a few years younger than him at the time. He shook her, and the boy ran at him, yelling. Panic made him shoot, and the next thing he knew was the boy writhing in pain, clutching his leg. They didn’t have the money to get help, and they all knew it. He threw up in the alley outside their apartment.
  • If the curse had never happened, what would you be doing right now?
    • Streaming, or if he got bored, leaving the planet to find something new to do. There’s only so much you can do in one city for your entire life, especially when you’re trying to avoid the consequences of your actions.

    • (raised in district where it then nuclear meltdown. Hard 2 get to)
  • Who were you closest to before The Scarring?
    • Ithax, Meskiv
  • Who do you still have left?
    • From his understanding, his dad is still around, though it’s a bit surprising. He’d texted him, something he did often asking for money or trying to check in with him, but all it said was “Don’t come home.”
  • What were some life changing memories before The Scarring?
    • The first few days after leaving home. He still hadn’t found a place to stay yet, and he hadn’t thought things through that much at his age. He ran out of food super quick, so he turned to asking people politely, and when that failed, he stole. It didn’t take Luthien long to find a kid willing to do anything for a place to stay.
  • Describe a moment from The Scarring’s first weeks that permanently changed you.
    • Ever since he’d moved away from Storm, he’d found peace in the anonymity of a new home and faces. But that peace shattered when someone followed him home and tried to stab him in the stairwell. He’d seen the fear on their face when he pulled a pistol out and shot with precision. His life or theirs, and he won. Guess he still had the reflexes, despite trying to avoid that kind of life.
