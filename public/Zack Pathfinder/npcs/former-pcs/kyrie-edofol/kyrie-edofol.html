Character Backstory

Taken in by Iustus Edofol in what is now Greenpaths when she was a child, she was brought to what is now Hols not long after. She grew up being a bookworm, not as interested in what they were doing. After nearly 3 decades of it, they forced her to travel the old empire and become helpful to the people who needed it most. She made friends from this, exposing her to different parts of the continent she never anticipated where she started getting strange dreams that left her confused and curious in the morning. They kept happening, until she began having dreams of people she'd grown close to dying to killers, disease, curses, and at the end: an offer of being able to help them in exchange for making a pact.

Settling into the pact wasn't too bad; she began sleepwalking, waking up to find herself in the center of town, which soon extended to the city next door as her power grew. After a few years, she'd wake up and find herself lost somewhere she'd never been before. Over the years, her patron became more and more pushy, pushing her toward and away certain things, only for her to end up being recruited for the follow-up expedition across The Pale. She got nightmares for days until she agreed to going.

Iustus never liked this pact that she made, and liked even less that she was leaving across the Pale so soon after Auctur fell since it was such an uncertain time.
